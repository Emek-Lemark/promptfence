# PromptFence Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** February 2025

## Summary

PromptFence (Personal) processes all data locally in your browser. **No data is collected, transmitted, or stored externally.**

## What Data We Process

PromptFence scans text you paste into AI chat prompts to detect sensitive information:
- Email addresses
- Phone numbers
- Bank account numbers (IBAN)
- Credit card numbers
- Physical addresses
- Passwords and API keys

## How We Process Data

1. **Local Processing Only**: All detection happens in your browser. Your clipboard content never leaves your device.
2. **No Network Requests**: The extension does not communicate with any external servers.
3. **No Analytics**: We do not collect usage statistics, telemetry, or any other data.
4. **No Accounts Required**: The extension works without any sign-up or login.

## Data Storage

- **Settings**: Your preset selection and toggle preferences are stored locally using Chrome's sync storage (synced across your Chrome browsers if you're signed in to Chrome).
- **No Content Storage**: We never store any clipboard content or detected sensitive data.

## Permissions Explained

| Permission | Why We Need It |
|------------|----------------|
| `storage` | Save your settings (preset, toggles) locally |
| Host permissions for AI sites | Run content scripts on ChatGPT, Claude, and Gemini to intercept paste events |

## Third Parties

PromptFence does not share any data with third parties because we don't collect any data.

## Changes to This Policy

We will update this policy if we make changes to how the extension handles data. The "Last updated" date at the top will reflect when changes were made.

## Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on our GitHub repository.
